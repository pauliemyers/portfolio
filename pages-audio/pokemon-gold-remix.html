Pokemon Gold: Wild Battle (Johto) Remix

Audio

Made using free virtual instruments and Reaper as the first assignment in an Audio Foundations class at New York University.

I originally wanted to create a 30 second piece that sounded like it "could" be from a Pokemon video game as part of a ringtone assignment. Halfway through, I realized that the theme that was in my head was actually very similar to a pre-existing theme. I decided to create a variation of the theme instead of an original piece, and I am satisfied with the results.
