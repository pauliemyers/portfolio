Bee Movie Found Sound Project

Audio

Made from scratch using an iPhone SE and Reaper as the first assignment in an Audio Foundations class at New York University.

I recorded myself speaking the beginning of the Bee Movie script with no sense of tone, and I added common household sound effects. The assignment was supposed to be experimental so students could learn how to use Reaper.
