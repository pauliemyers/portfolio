Elastic Heart Remix

Audio

Made from a multi-track version of Sia's Elastic Heart, my own voice, and Reaper as part of the second assignment for an Audio Foundations Class at New York University.

I made a short, rough remix of Sia's Elastic Heart to complete a remixing assignment. I isolated the chorus' drums to make them stand out, as I thought they were an interesting part of the original song that I hadn't noticed until navigating the individual tracks.
