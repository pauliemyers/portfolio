The Professor's Photobomb

Photo Manipulation

Edited in Fall 2014 as part of a Digital Foundations class at the University of Connecticut.

The assignment called for students to choose from a selection of pictures of our professor (far left) in front of a greenscreen to edit into another photograph. I went for a comedic approach.
