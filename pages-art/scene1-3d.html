Composition Assignment Part II

3D modeling

Made using Maya 2016 and Mudbox 2016. Part 1 of a child's bedroom composition.

This was the final project in a 3D modeling class at New York University. Our instructor asked us to create, light, and render a full 3D scene. Students were also told to search online for free 3D assets and then present on where those assets could be found. His goal was to show how taking advantage of free resources could greatly increase the quality of our work.
