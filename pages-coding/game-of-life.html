Game of Life

p5.js

Created in p5.JS as part of a midterm assignment in a Creative Coding class at New York University.


NOTE: This script will produce flashing lights at a faster-than-normal pace. If you are photosensitive, please leave this page.


The assignment was to edit a Game of Life simulation provided to us by our professor. He asked us to find self-sustaining systems other than the one he provided us and to experiment with the visualization of the sustaining system.
