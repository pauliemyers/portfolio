Spirograph

p5.js

Created in p5.JS as part of a midterm assignment in a Creative Coding class at New York University.
NOTE: This script will produce sounds at a high and low frequency. Please lower your volume.
The assignment was to edit a spirograph given to us by the professor to incorporate sound and to make it more interesting. My assignment has two modes: a primary mode and a tracing mode. In the trace mode, an image is created. When returning to the primary mode, the image of the trace is saved to the folder in which the script is located.
