p5.js

Created in p5.JS and p5.play as part of a final assignment in a Creative Coding class at New York University.

This game is in-progress, and it is a slow, short 2D platformer/walking simulator in which a character walks and brings color to a bleached environment with each footstep. At the end, the character is faced with the open air, and the player is forced to decide whether to venture into the future or to return to the comfort of the past. All sprites were created by me.
