The Cyoub

Design

Presentation created in Powerpoint 2016 as the first assignment for an Ideation and Prototyping Class at New York University.



Students were assigned a project to experiment with their own design methodology. Over the course of several weeks, we were tasked to create a home solution for a specific audience living in New York City.

The Cyoub is meant to be a storage solution for people living without a lot of storage or floor space. It is a modifiable storage cube that can hang at varied heights from supports in the closet or along walls, or it can stack into wall frames or even on top of another Cyoub. It's the Storage Cube that works for You.
