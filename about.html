About

Paul is a student at New York University studying Integrated Digital Media. He is pursuing research in virtual reality and its applications within healthcare, he is working on small game projects on the side, and he has made this portfolio as the final assignment for an Introduction to Web Development class.


In his free time, Paul is likes to play board games and video games, listen to, perform, and arrange music, travel, try new food, and go birdwatching. He is interested in producing LGBT-friendly and inclusive content as a designer of digital media, learning how to live and succeed in NYC, and all things Australia.

Paul is not currently on any social media, but as those profiles develop, links will be provided on this page.
